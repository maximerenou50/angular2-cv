Hyperloop

Hyperloop is a transportation project launched by Elon Musk (CEO of Tesla Motors) to be the fifth way of moving, after planes, trains, boats and cars. The idea is to move some capsules in low pressure tubes. The tubes will have solar panels on them so they will be self-powered. This new way of moving will be faster (up to 760 mph) and better for environment than airplanes. It might also be cheaper than train (Elon Musk said the cost to Hyperloop between San Francisco and Los Angeles will be 6 billions against the 60 billions for the California High-Speed Rail).

What I like about this project is the futurist part but also the realistic part. This kind of projects might revolutionized the way of moving, which make easier traveling.
